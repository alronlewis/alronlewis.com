About Me

Hi, I’m Alron Lewis, a self-taught traditional artist since 2020. I specialize in graphite medium and enjoy drawing both realistic and anime-style art. With years of experience, I have mastered the art of drawing and try to learn different styles. I believe art is not just a hobby; it's a way of life.

A showcase of my journey — each piece tells a story beyond the paper.
