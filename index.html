About Me

Artist: Alron Lewis

I am Alron Lewis, a self-taught traditional artist since 2020. I specialize in graphite medium and enjoy drawing both anime and realistic portraits. Over the years, I have mastered the art of drawing by learning to show the emotions and even the finest details in my work using my imagination. Art is more than a hobby for me; it is a way of life that fuels my desire for self-improvement.
